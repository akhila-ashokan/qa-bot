Get to Know Our Faculty
Our faculty are at the forefront of research in catalysis and surface chemistry, biomolecular engineering, and soft materials. Their faculty features are profiles that detail their motivations, background, and area of research. Their faculty research profiles will give you a deeper look into their specializations and research questions. You can also reference their directory listings for more information or to get in touch.
