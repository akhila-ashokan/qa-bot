Community Liaisons are student leaders committed to community building, public engagement, learning, and service. OCCL Community Liaisons assume an active leadership in and among students living in non-university owned or certified housing. Above all Community Liaisons are community builders and peer educators who help fellow students to become responsible, engaged members of the university and surrounding communities.
