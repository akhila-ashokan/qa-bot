Request information
Request information or submit questions by e-mailing us.
Schedule an appointment
Make an appointment by calling (217) 333-0112. Prior to making an appointment please view our website. It provides a wide range of information and resources that may be helpful as you consider the questions or concerns that you may want to address. In this way appointment time can be spent on a specific area of concern not addressed on the website.
Attend or request a workshop
Our team of community liaisons present on a variety of topics such as lease education, tenant rights and responsibilities, campus and community safety, roommate issues, etc. You can request a workshop online.
