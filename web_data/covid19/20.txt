On-Campus COVID-19 Testing Locations
A number of free on-campus COVID-19 walk-up testing locations are available to current faculty, staff and students across campus who have active NetIDs and i-cards. 
Questions about testing and results
The University of Illinois COVID Wellness Answer Center is available to answer questions about on-campus testing and results. 
Please contact the COVID Wellness Answer Center by email at covidwellness@illinois.edu or call 217-333-1900.
Current testing locations
All testing sites have fully accessible wheelchair ramps from the sidewalk or gravel into the tents. If you have difficulty with accessibility at any testing site, please Report a Barrier so concerns can be addressed immediately.
Parking
For those who wish to park closer to their destination, you may use metered/app parking where available.
