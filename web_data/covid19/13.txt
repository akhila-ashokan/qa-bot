College and Unit Information – COVID-19
The COVID-19 pandemic has effected all parts of the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. A number of colleges, departments, institutes and other campus units are providing updates about how COVID-19 is affecting their unit’s operations.  
Each unit and several divisions within some units have identified at least one individual to be their COVID-19 Coordinator for return to on-site coordination and planning. 

Please consider supporting COVID relief efforts, including the SHIELD initiative or direct student funding, by making a gift  today!
