SHIELD CU – COVID-19
SHIELD CU is a partnership between the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and OSF HealthCare. 
Community members, family members and visitors can test for COVID-19 through SHIELD CU. The program is built around the saliva COVID-19 testing developed and implemented at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign
Testing is free for the general public and available for those age 5 and older.
Please visit the SHIELD CU website for more information, including testing locations and hours.
