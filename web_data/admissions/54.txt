Choosing Your Residence Hall
UIUC offers 24 University Housing facilities, which are owned by the university, and 13 Private Certified Housing facilities, which are privately owned and operated. 
University Housing
These 24 living options span across campus and include 11 Living-Learning Communities (LLCs) centering around common themes. Those interested in University Housing should complete their contract by May 15.
Explore UH Options
Private Certified Housing
These 13 living options are also approved by the university for new students. Each unit has its own application process, and deadlines to guarantee space vary. If you're interested in a specific hall, contact them directly for more information.
