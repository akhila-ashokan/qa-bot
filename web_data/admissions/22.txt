Professors, Undergraduate Admissions, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign
Our professors are renowned in their fields and constantly finding new ways to change the world through their teaching and research.
UIUC faculty members are accessible, even outside of the classroom. You can attend their office hours to discuss topics from class and network for research or internship opportunities.
We have too many great professors and too little space, but here are a few worth noting.
Journalism
A former Washington Post reporter and Pulitzer Prize–winning author for his work on poverty and crime in Washington, D.C.
Physics
A 2015 national professor of the year and particle physics specialist who has put his passion for physics education toward the development of the iClicker and other interactive learning tools.
Voice
A main-stage soprano at the Metropolitan Opera for 15 years who's shared the stage with the likes of Placido Domingo, Luciano Pavarotti, and Renée Fleming.
Business Administration
Uses research and teaching to help direct the world toward sustainable marketplaces, believing "it is essential to do good in order to do well."
Professor Michael Raycraft takes his Recreation, Sport, and Tourism students on a yearly trip, giving them an opportunity to meet professionals in their field and explore potential career options.
