Champaign-Urbana, Undergraduate Admissions, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign
The microurban twin cities of Champaign and Urbana combine to form a vibrant, innovative community in which our campus is the heart. As you explore the many perks of big-city living in this relaxed setting, you’ll quickly understand why we earn regular honors as a top college town.
(Livability)
Living local is the culture in C-U, with 2 distinct downtowns that will enhance your campus experience. Discover live music, festivals, theaters, boutiques, and over 50 locally owned restaurants that can be enjoyed throughout the year.

Explore C-U
Something's always happening in C-U. Get familiar with some of our yearly highlights.
Rooted in music and happening each September across C-U, a festival that's expanded to include literature, makers, and local food—and it's still growing!
A Boston Marathon qualifier bringing thousands of runners from across the nation and beyond to C-U.
Held on Mom's Weekend, a county-wide festival featuring hundreds of local visual and music artists across a variety of venues.
A film festival created by film critic and UIUC alum Roger Ebert, hosted at the historic Virginia Theater in downtown Champaign.
Located in downtown Urbana, one of the largest and most diverse farmers’ markets in the state of Illinois.
