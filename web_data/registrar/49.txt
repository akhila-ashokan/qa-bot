Calendars
Need to reference dates related to the Office of the Registrar? Review our calendars below.
Office of the Registrar Master Calendar
The complete Office of the Registrar Master Calendar includes comprehensive information about deadlines and activities for all units within the Office of the Registrar.
Tools are available to export events to other Outlook calendars or request RSS feeds for updates. Many links are included to provide additional details about the deadlines or events. Results can be displayed for a term by entering the term (e.g., “Fall 2012”) in the search field.
Academic Calendars
For academic calendars containing registration periods and official academic dates, including the deadlines for adding and dropping classes, go to Academic Calendars.
Processing Calendars
For processing calendars relating to Facility Management and Scheduling in the Office of the Registrar, go to Course Catalog and Scheduling.
