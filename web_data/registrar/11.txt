Auditing
Information about auditor status is described in detail in Section 3-305 of the Student Code.
Allow up to 2 weeks for processing time after the Auditor’s Permit is submitted before students will have access to online resources.
Students should be advised to bring or email the completed Auditor’s Permit, which is issued by the graduate department or undergraduate college office, to the Records Service Center in the Admissions and Records Building, 901 West Illinois Street, Suite 140, Urbana, IL 61801 for processing.
