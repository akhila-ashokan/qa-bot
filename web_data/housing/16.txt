Staff & Employment
Diverse, dedicated, dynamic and innovative—these are just a few words to describe the staff of University Housing. More than 800 skilled professionals create a safe and welcoming home for our residents every day. 
We also employ more than 1,500 students every year to keep our operation running smoothly. Student job opportunities include dining hall and catering workers, computer support, desk clerks, graphic designers, resident advisors, conference staff, graduate assistants, and more.
