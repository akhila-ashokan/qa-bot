Scholarships

	Scholarships are a form of gift aid that doesn’t require repayment. They’re generally awarded on a variety of factors, including academic achievement, talent, athletic ability, leadership, geographical location, field of study, or financial need.

Merit-Based Scholarships

When you apply to Illinois, you’re automatically considered for our merit-based scholarships. Many of these are for freshmen and transfers in any college based on academic excellence, though awards are also given out based on major.
