Office of Student Financial Aid
Waivers

	Be aware of the waivers Illinois awards to reduce the cost of your tuition.

Child of Employee Tuition Waiver

	If you are the child of a university employee, you may be eligible for a 50% tuition waiver.

Children of Veterans Tuition Waiver

	If you are a child of a veteran, you may be eligible for a tuition waiver.

Graduate Assistantship Tuition Waiver

	Do you have a graduate assistantship? Read our tuition waiver policy.

Senior Citizen Courses Act Tuition Waiver

	If you are 65 years of age or older, you may be qualified to apply for a waiver.

Policy

	Learn the policies we have in place for waivers administered through our office.
