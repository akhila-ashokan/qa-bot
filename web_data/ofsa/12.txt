Types of Aid – Office of Student Financial Aid
Office of Student Financial Aid
Types of Aid

	Our office helps students afford educational expenses by awarding aid from federal, state, university, and private resources.

Grants

	You may be given aid based on demonstrated financial need.

Scholarships

	You may be awarded aid based on such factors as academic achievement, talent, athletic ability, or field of study.

Waivers

	You may be exempt from certain university fees based on specific cases.

Loans

	You can borrow funds to help pay for your educational expenses.

Employment

	You can earn money toward your education by working during college.

Other Aid

Other types of aid include international aid, veteran aid, tax credits, and pre-paid tuition.
