Children of Veterans Tuition Waiver
The Children of Veterans Tuition Waiver is a four-year consecutive in-state tuition waiver at the University of Illinois given to natural, step, or legally adopted children of service members who are veterans.
How to Apply
Fill out and submit the Children of Veterans Tuition Waiver Form.  The Priority Deadline for 2022 – 2023 is January 5, 2022 and all applicants are notified by March 9, 2022.  The application deadline for conflicts not awarded closes October 1, 2022.
