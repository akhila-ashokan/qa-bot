Grants – Office of Student Financial Aid
Office of Student Financial Aid
Grants
Grants are awarded based on demonstrated financial need. They don’t need to be repaid.
All grants have different eligibility requirements. Eligibility for need-based grants is determined using federal or state formulas and the information you provide on your FAFSA, which you must complete in order to qualify for the following programs:
The Illinois Student Assistance Commission also administers several grants. If you are not a resident of the State of Illinois, contact your home state grant agency to identify any additional grant funds you may be eligible for.
